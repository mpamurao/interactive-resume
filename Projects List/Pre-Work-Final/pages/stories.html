Anime

One of my favorite hobbies is watching anime. The first show I remember watching was Pokemon. I followed the series from the beginning when the main character Ash received Pikachu, his first Pokemon. As the world continued to expand with new Pokemon and evolutions, I eventually stopped watching after the season with "Battle Frontier". My first video game was also Pokemon, specifically Fire Red and Leaf Green for the Gameboy Advance SP.

I thought the adventures in the Pokemon world were exciting and contained meaningful relationships and memories that Ash made along his journey. My love for Pokemon as a kid eventually led me to watching other anime and playing more video games.

Ironically, my favorite show isn't Pokemon. It's Clannad.
